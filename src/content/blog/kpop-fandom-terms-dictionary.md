# K-Pop Fandom Terms Dictionary: The Ultimate Guide

New to K-Pop? Confused by all the unique terminology fans use? Don't worry! This comprehensive dictionary will help you understand every term, from basic vocabulary to advanced fandom slang.

## Essential Terms (Must Know!)

### Bias & Related Terms

| Term | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Bias** | Your favorite member in a group | "Jimin is my bias in BTS" |
| **Bias wrecker** | Member who makes you question your bias | "V keeps bias-wrecking me!" |
| **Ultimate bias** | Your #1 favorite idol across ALL groups | "My ult is Jennie" |
| **Multi-stan** | Fan who stans multiple groups | Common in modern K-Pop |
| **OT(number)** | Supporting all members equally (e.g., OT7 for BTS) | "I'm OT5 for NewJeans" |

### Fan Identity Terms

| Term | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Stan** | Dedicated fan (from "stalker + fan") | "I stan BLACKPINK" |
| **Casual fan** | Enjoys music but not deeply invested | Likes the songs, doesn't buy albums |
| **Akgae** | Fan of only one member (often toxic) | Negative term |
| **Sasaeng** | Obsessive fan who invades privacy | Very negative term |
| **Anti** | Someone who actively hates a group/idol | Opposite of fan |

## Music & Performance Terms

### Album Types

| Term | Meaning | Typical Tracks |
|------|---------|----------------|
| **Full album** | Complete album release | 10-14 songs |
| **Mini album / EP** | Shorter collection | 4-7 songs |
| **Single album** | 1-3 songs only | Focus on title track |
| **Repackage** | Re-release with bonus tracks | Original + 2-3 new |
| **Special album** | Holiday/anniversary release | Varies |
| **Pre-release** | Song released before album | Teaser for album |

### Song Types

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Title track** | Main promoted song |
| **B-side** | Non-title tracks on album |
| **Double title** | Two songs promoted equally |
| **Intro/Outro** | Opening/closing album tracks |
| **Unit song** | Subset of members singing |
| **OST** | Original Soundtrack (drama/movie) |

### Performance Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Comeback** | New music release + promotions |
| **Debut** | First official release as group |
| **Comeback stage** | First performance of new song |
| **Encore stage** | Winning group performs again |
| **Fan chant** | Organized cheering during songs |
| **Point choreography** | Signature dance move |
| **Center** | Member positioned in middle |
| **Killing part** | Most memorable song/dance section |
