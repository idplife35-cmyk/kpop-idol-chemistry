❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What defines an aesthetic name?

Consistent mood and phonetics—soft vowels for gentle vibes, strong consonants for dark styles, elegant endings for dreamy looks.

Should I prioritize looks or readability?

Balance both. Names should look great and be easy to pronounce across audiences.

Can I mix languages?

Yes, minimal French/Latin touches are common. Avoid overcomplication for handles and search.
