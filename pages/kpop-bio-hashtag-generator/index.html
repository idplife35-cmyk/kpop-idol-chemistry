❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long should a bio be?

One concise line (60–120 chars) works best. Put emojis first to signal vibe.

How many hashtags per post?

8–15 relevant tags outperform walls of tags. Rotate bundles; avoid repetition spam.

Any platform cautions?

Avoid banned/overused tags and aggressive repetition; it can reduce reach or trigger filters.

Should bios and handles match?

Use consistent tone/keywords so people recognize you across platforms.
