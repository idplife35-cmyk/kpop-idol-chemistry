❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a name cute?

Softer vowels, repeating sounds, and gentle endings (‑a/‑i/‑rin). 2–3 syllables feel bouncy and friendly.

Can cute names still be unique?

Yes—mix rare syllables or add subtle twists (Roa, Mirae) to stand out while staying sweet.

Are hyphens okay?

Hyphens help readability for multi‑syllable cute names; for handles, test availability first.
