❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What’s a good nickname length?

2–6 characters. Shorter is more memorable and handle‑friendly.

Should it match my bias or group?

It can. Many fans include subtle references; keep it readable for non‑fans too.

Can I mix Hangul and Latin?

Yes. Mixed scripts can look stylish—ensure it’s easy to type on your platforms.

Do I need unique characters?

Optional. Symbols can add style but may reduce searchability. Test on your platform first.
