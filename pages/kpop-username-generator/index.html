❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I ensure availability?

Prepare 3–5 variants; test on your platform. Add subtle suffixes (hq, _official, .studio).

What length works best?

10–16 characters are readable on most UIs. Avoid long sequences of numbers or symbols.

Underscore or dot?

Both are fine. Dots feel clean on IG; underscores are common on TikTok and X.

Should it match my bio/hashtags?

Yes—keep a consistent vibe across handle, bio line, and tag bundle for brand cohesion.
