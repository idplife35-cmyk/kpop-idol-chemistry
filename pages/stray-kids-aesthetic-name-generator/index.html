❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a name Stray Kids aesthetic?

Stray Kids aesthetic names are bold, experimental, and powerful—embracing edgy sounds, unconventional combinations, and fearless self-expression with raw impact.

Should I go for dark or powerful?

Both! Stray Kids balances edge with power. Mix dark words (Void, Shadow, Noir) with impact words (Blaze, Thunder, Storm) for authentic SKZ energy.

Can I use member names?

Yes! Use Chan, Minho (Lee Know), Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, I.N (Jeongin) or surnames (Bang, Lee, Seo, Hwang, Han, Kim, Yang) for STAY names.

Best for music/producer accounts?

Choose experimental elements like Cipher, Glitch, Frequency, Wave, Pulse—reflecting Stray Kids's self-producing artistry and innovative sound.
