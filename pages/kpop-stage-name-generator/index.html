❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a strong stage name?

Short length, punchy syllables, and distinct consonants. Names should be memorable and easy to chant.

Can I use Korean or English forms?

Yes. Many idols use English‑influenced or mixed forms. Choose what fits your concept and audience.

Should it match my concept?

Absolutely. Align tone (power, elegant, dark) with your performance style and branding.

Can I change later?

Stage names often evolve. Keep a consistent handle for socials while you experiment.
