❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How are ship names created?

Ship names are usually blends of two idol names (first syllables or distinctive parts). Fans often choose the catchiest and most memorable blend.

Are these official pairings?

No. Ship names are fan‑made nicknames for fun and creative purposes. They are not official or endorsed by artists or agencies.

Can I generate ship names for any pairing?

Yes. Use the main generator to explore names by group and then craft a blend, or use dedicated combiner pages to mash two names.

Will I always get the same result?

Our generators are seeded so the same inputs tend to produce consistent output, which helps you save and share favorite combinations.

Can I use ship names in my stories and socials?

Absolutely—use them for fanfiction, roleplay, and profiles. Please be respectful of artists and fandom guidelines.
