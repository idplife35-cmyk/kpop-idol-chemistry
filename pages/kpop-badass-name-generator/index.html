❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What phonetics feel badass?

Strong consonants (k, t, x, v), short stressed syllables, and sharp endings add impact.

Is shorter always better?

Usually 2–3 syllables work best for punch and memorability, especially on stage.

Can I use symbols or numbers?

Use sparingly; they can look edgy but may harm searchability and handle availability.

How do I keep it appropriate?

Avoid explicit terms; focus on mood (dark/power/tech) rather than profanity.
