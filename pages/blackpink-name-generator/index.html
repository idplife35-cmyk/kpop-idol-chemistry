🌟 Group Overview

BLACKPINK (블랙핑크) is a South Korean girl group known for their powerful performances and global impact. The name represents the contrast between "BLACK" (strong, powerful) and "PINK" (beautiful, feminine), symbolizing their dual nature.

🎵 Musical Style

  • Hip-hop & EDM: Strong beats and powerful vocals
  • Bold concepts: Confident and empowering messages
  • International appeal: Global hits and collaborations
  • Visual excellence: Stunning music videos and performances

FAQ

Answers to common questions about how it works.

How are the names generated?

Our generator creates two unique Korean names for you:

  • Same-name: Combines a Korean surname with your chosen idol's name (like Kim Jennie)
  • Styled-name: Creates a completely new name that matches your relationship type and gender preference

The same inputs will always give you the same results, so you can save your favorite combinations!

What is the chemistry score?

A fun, seeded score between 70–100 based on your inputs. It's not a real compatibility measure.

How does the Gender option work?

Choose the gender style that best fits your vision:

  • Male: Creates more masculine-sounding Korean names
  • Female: Generates more feminine-sounding Korean names
  • Auto: Automatically detects the best style based on your name and chosen idol
How do I search idols? What's included?

Simply type the idol's name in Korean or English! Our smart search will help you find the perfect match.

This page focuses on BLACKPINK members, but you can also try our other group generators for BTS, NewJeans, IVE, HUNTR/X, and SajaBoys. Don't worry about spelling - our search is forgiving!

Privacy: is anything stored or sent?

Everything runs in your browser. We don't upload your inputs to a server.

  • Only your language preference is saved in localStorage.
Why show both Korean and English names?

This site supports international users. English names are romanized using a simplified mapping.

Can I use this offline? Any requirements?

Yes! Once the page loads, everything works in your browser. No internet connection needed after the initial load.

Just make sure you're using a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge for the best experience.

How do I change language?

Switch between English and 한국어 using the selector at the top-right—content updates instantly without reloading.
