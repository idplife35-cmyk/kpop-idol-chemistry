🇰🇷 Korean Naming Traditions

Korean names typically consist of a family name (성) followed by a given name (이름). Most Korean family names are one syllable, while given names are usually two syllables.

  • • Common surnames: 김 (Kim), 이 (Lee), 박 (Park), 최 (Choi)
  • • Meaningful names: Often relate to nature, virtues, or aspirations
  • • Gender patterns: Different sounds and meanings for boys vs. girls
🎤 K-Pop Stage Names

Many K-Pop idols use stage names that are easier to pronounce internationally or have special meanings related to their artistic identity.
